LIFESTYLE CHECKER

What is it ?

Welcome to our Lifestyle Calculator, designed to help you take control of your finances and ensure a secure financial future. Our calculator takes into account your financial expectations and calculates if you have enough money to meet these expectations. This tool provides a simple and effective way to plan your budget and make informed decisions about your finances. With our Lifestyle Calculator, you can take the first step towards realizing your financial goals and securing a brighter financial future. So why wait? Start using our calculator today and get a clearer picture of your financial situation.

How should you use the Lifestyle checker

The lifestyle checker includes the 7 main areas of household spending. The average cost has been included in each section along with some useful links. All you need to do is select whether this is right for your current or desired spending or if you will spend more or less. Once you choose your amounts and submit your monthly salary the result will show how much excess you have left from your salary each month and what will help you to understand what salary you need to achieve your desired lifestyle along with some useful tips to help you achieve this.
